Bit-Get

Display and monitor the currency market on your homescreen.

Set customizable refresh intervals and receive alerts when the market matches your conditions.

Keep track of most currencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Ripple...

  1. Download and install Bit-Get
  2. Add Bit-Get widget to your homescreen
  3. Open Bit-Get application to configure Bit-Get widget

Note : This application lets you customize the contents of the widget.

Widget will not refresh properly while using power saving mode.
